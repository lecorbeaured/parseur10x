How to Fix Your Credit Score Fast: 7 Strategies That Actually Work

Published March 28, 2026 · PARSEUR 10X Team · 8 min read

Your credit score can improve faster than you think — if you focus on the right things. Here are seven proven strategies ranked by impact, starting with the fastest wins.

1. Dispute Errors and Inaccuracies

This is the single fastest way to boost your score. If there's wrong information on your report, removing it can add 50-150 points overnight. Here's how to dispute. Even better — let PARSEUR 10X find the errors and generate your dispute letters automatically.

2. Pay Down High Utilization

Credit utilization (how much of your available credit you're using) accounts for about 30% of your score. If you're above 30%, paying down balances can produce immediate results. Target below 10% for maximum impact. This change reflects on your next statement date.

3. Remove Collection Accounts

Debt validation and pay-for-delete can remove collections that are dragging your score down. A single removed collection can add 50-100+ points.

4. Become an Authorized User

Ask a family member or trusted friend with excellent credit to add you as an authorized user on their oldest, lowest-utilization card. Their positive payment history gets added to your report. You don't even need to use the card.

5. Add Credit Builder Tradelines

Products like Kikoff and Ava Credit add positive payment history to your report every month. This is especially impactful if you have a thin credit file (few accounts).

6. Request Goodwill Removals

If you have late payments on otherwise good accounts, a goodwill letter can get them removed. This works best for isolated late payments where you have a long positive history.

7. Limit Hard Inquiries

Each hard inquiry costs 5-10 points. If you have several recent inquiries, stop applying for new credit and let them age. They fall off after 2 years and stop affecting your score after about 12 months.
