Free Credit Report Parser: Let AI Analyze Your Report

How It Works

Step 1: Upload your credit report PDF from any bureau (Equifax, Experian, or TransUnion). Reports from AnnualCreditReport.com, Credit Karma, and IdentityIQ all work.

Step 2: AI reads every line and identifies negative items — late payments, collections, charge-offs, high utilization, excessive inquiries, and more.

Step 3: Each item gets an impact score showing how much it's hurting you, plus a recommended fix strategy with a detailed explanation.
